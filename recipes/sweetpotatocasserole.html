Sweet Potato Casserole

Baked Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Topping

You'll be sure to impress all your guests during a festive holiday celebration or day-to-day sit-down dinner with this delicious sweet potato casserole. Its pecan topping will make your stomach growl and mouth water. Be careful, as it is quite addictive - you've been warned!

Ingredients

Sweet Potatoes

  • 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Pecan Topping

  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ½ cup chopped pecans

Steps

Sweet Potatoes

  1. Preheat oven (325 degrees F/165 degrees C).
  2. Place sweet potatoes in a medium saucepan and cover with water. Set stove to medium-high heat and cook potatoes for 10-15 minutes until tender. Drain and place in a large bowl.
  3. Mash sweet potatoes with a fork. Add eggs; mix well. Add sugar, milk, butter, vanilla, and salt; mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Pecan Topping

  1. Mix brown sugar and flour together in a medium bowl. Cut in butter until mixture is coarse. Stir in pecans.
  2. Sprinkle topping over sweet potato mixture.
  3. Bake in oven for about 30 minutes until topping is lightly browned.
