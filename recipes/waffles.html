    Homemade Waffles

    Wake up and whip out your waffle iron because it's time to make some waffles! This is a quick and easy recipe containing ingredients you probably keep in your kitchen pantry. Get fancy and add some ice cream or stay traditional and stick with a simple drizzle of maple syrup.

    Ingredients

    • 2 large eggs
    • 3 cups all-purposeflour
    • 1 ¾ cups milk
    • ½ cup vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon white sugar
    • 4 teaspoons baking powder
    • ¼ teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
    • nonstick cooking spray

    Steps

    1. Preheat waffle iron.
    2. Whisk eggs until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine. Whisk in sugar, then add baking powder, salt, and vanilla and mix until smooth.
    3. Spray waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto iron and cook for 3-5 minutes until golden brown.
