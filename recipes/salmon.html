Brown Sugar Glazed Salmon

Are you in the mood to make a yummy dinner that all your family members will love? This simple salmon recipe is bursting with flavor and only takes 15 minutes to make! We recommend serving with rice and a vegetable of your choice.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • Â¼ cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat oven broiler and place rack approx. 6 inches from heat source. Apply cooking spray to rack.
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper before placing it on the broiler pan.
  3. Mix brown sugar and mustard and spread evenly on salmon.
  4. Broil for 10-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
